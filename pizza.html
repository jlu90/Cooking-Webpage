Whole Wheat Veggie Pizzas



When a pizza craving hits, there is nothing that can be done but to eat pizza! While a frozen pizza or delivery, will certainly fit the bill, sometimes, I crave something a little healthier and lighter. Luckily, these mini pizzas are made with whole wheat pizza dough, fresh cheese, and lots of veggies. They're perfect for satisfying a pizza craving without being too heavy.

Basic Information:
  • Makes: 4 mini pizzas
  • Total Cook Time: ~45 minutes (includes prep)
What You Will Need:
  • Whole Wheat Pizza Dough (I love this one from Cookie+Kate)
  • 14 oz. canned diced tomatoes (I prefer Muir Glen)
  • 4 tsp Italian herbs seasoning
  • 4 oz. fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings, such as bell peppers, basil, or eggplant
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated parmesan cheese
How to Make It:
  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F
  2. Divide the pizza dough into four equal balls
  3. On a lightly floured surface, roll the balls into four discs
  4. Lightly brush olive oil on the dough
  5. Gently spoon diced tomatoes onto the dough
  6. Sprinkle 1 tsp of italian herbs seasoning onto the tomatoes
  7. Top with slices of fresh mozzarella and toppings of your choice
  8. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and place pizzas on baking sheet
  9. Bake until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly (approximately 10 to 12 minutes)
  10. Remove pizzas from the oven and garnish with a sprinkle of sea salt, freshly cracked pepper, and freshly grated parmesan cheese
  11. Enjoy! :)
